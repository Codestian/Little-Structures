## About

The Little Miniature Recipe allows you to make a LT miniature out of vanilla Minecraft mods, in any grid scale.

Almost all full blocks can be miniaturized. Things like stairs and slabs will be ignored in the conversion.

## How to use

1. First build something you want to make a miniature out of Minecraft blocks. For example, let's use this simple house:

![2018-05-10_23 36 30](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/29471180/39904307-97e6bb66-54ad-11e8-9de3-9b132b0b56a9.png)

2. Then, using the Little Miniature Recipe, right click both corners of the build you wish to miniaturize. This works the same way as a [Little Recipe](https://github.com/CreativeMD/LittleTiles/wiki/Little-Recipe):


![miniature recipe01](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/29471180/39903897-c94783ea-54ab-11e8-924a-ccdb2c2e43d4.gif)

3. Use the Wrench to extract the miniature from the Little Miniature Recipe:

![miniature recipe02](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/29471180/39903899-ca271226-54ab-11e8-93a2-dd41235aba85.gif)

## Tips and Tricks

* Building in grids lower than 64 can be tricky. The Little Miniature Recipe can be particularly useful for making structures in small scales. Just make the miniature using normal Minecraft blocks then miniaturize it!
